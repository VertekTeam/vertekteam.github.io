What is VertekOS 10?

VertekOS 10 is a lightweight, unbranded version of Windows 10 LTSC 2021. Designed to offer stable performance, it's ideal for:

  • Gaming setups – fewer background apps and processes
  • Old or low-spec PCs – minimal resource usage
  • Virtual machines or test environments

Quick Specs

  • Based on Windows 10 LTSC 2021 (no Store, Cortana, telemetry, etc.)
  • Local account: VertekOS User
  • PC name: VertekOS PC
  • No custom branding – standard Windows look
